    HOW TO PLAY

    1. Allow your camera — you become the diver! Wave your hands (both work!) to pop bubbles. No camera? Just tap.
    2. Pop white O₂ bubbles to survive. +5 Chain them fast for a COMBO — x2 x3 x4 multiplier!
    3. Jellyfish hide as pretty bubbles — pop 3 and you're out. ☠
    4. Golden bubble ✨ is rare & fast — grab it for +50
    5. Gift bubbles 🎁 drop points, an extra ♥, or a power-up: FRENZY (all O₂), SLOW-MO, or a SHIELD that eats one jellyfish hit.
    6. Level up! 250 → Lv2, 500 → Lv3. It gets deeper, darker & faster, and jellyfish turn angry. Dodge the BOSS JELLY for +30.
    7. Reach 700 to WIN 🏆 — but first survive the FINAL BOSS and its vicious jelly swarm!
