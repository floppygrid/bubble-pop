HOW TO PLAY

  1. Allow your camera. You become the diver — wave your hands to touch things on screen!
  2. Pop white O₂ bubbles with your fingertips — use both hands! (Or just tap them.) Each one keeps you breathing. +5
  3. Beware of jellyfish! They disguise themselves as pretty pink & blue bubbles. Pop 3 and it's game over. ☠
  4. Gift bubbles hide treasure — bonus points +10 +25 and rarely an extra life ♥
  5. The deeper you go, the faster it gets… and the jellyfish multiply!
!
